Frequently Asked Questions

Get the answers to your most common questions about the eCourt platform.

How do I register as a Citizen?

To register as a citizen, visit the registration page, provide your personal details, and create an account.

What is the role of a Judge in the eCourt system?

Judges are responsible for overseeing cases, delivering judgments, and ensuring the fair administration of justice.

How can I submit a case?

To submit a case, you must be logged in. After logging in, go to the "Submit Case" section and fill in the required details.

How is my personal information protected?

We implement stringent security measures to ensure your personal data is protected and securely stored.
