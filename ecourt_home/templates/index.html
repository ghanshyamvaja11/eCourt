Frequently Asked Questions

What is eCourt?

eCourt is an online platform that modernizes the court management process, enabling users to manage cases, hearings, and filings easily and efficiently.

How do I register?

You can register on our platform by filling out the signup form for your role (Citizen, Lawyer, Judge, or Admin).

Can I file a case online?

Yes, citizens can file a new case through the eCourt platform by submitting the necessary documents online.

Is my data safe?

Yes, eCourt uses high-level encryption and security measures to ensure the privacy and safety of all user data.

Can I track my case status?

Yes, users can track the status of their cases and hearings through their dashboard.

How do I submit legal documents?

Lawyers and citizens can submit legal documents via the eFiling system on the platform.

Who can see my documents?

Only authorized users such as judges, lawyers, and relevant parties can access case-related documents.

Can I cancel or reschedule a hearing?

Only the assigned judge can cancel or reschedule a hearing. However, lawyers can request changes through the platform.

How do I contact support?

You can contact our support team through the Contact Us page.
